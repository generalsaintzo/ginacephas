ABOUT

Gina Cephas, born Egbe Regina Mbeng on March 15, 1988, is a Nigerian songwriter and singer known for her blend of pop, hip hop, dancehall, and rap music. She was born in Ikom, Cross River State, and moved to Lagos to pursue her music career. Gina started composing songs at a young age and released her debut single "Super Giver" in 2014. She signed a recording contract with Cephas Music Entertainment and has gained recognition for her singles "Gimme Chance" and "Sugar Baby".
